About Me

Hi, I'm Prabhanjana, a Statistics and Computer Science undergrad at Christ University, Bangalore. I gravitate toward problems where the data is messy and the structure has to be earned.

My areas of expertise include machine learning, data engineering, and geospatial analysis. I have built OCR and NLP pipelines, knowledge graphs, routing-based GIS systems, and developer tooling, and I publish research alongside the things I build.

Outside of data, I have spent five years making 3D art in Blender, which is where the visuals on this site come from.
